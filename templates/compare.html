◈ HOW SCORES ARE CALCULATED ▼
🔥 LOYALTY SCORE
A score out of 100 measuring how devoted you are to an artist.

40% — Completion rate (how often you finish their songs)
30% — Total plays (capped at 100 plays = full marks)
20% — Longest listening streak (capped at 30 days)
10% — Active listening days (capped at 50 days)

A score of 100 means you listen constantly, never skip, and have long streaks.
📅 CONSISTENCY %
How regularly you listen to an artist relative to how long you've known them.

Calculated as: (active days ÷ days since first play) × 100

A score of 100% means you listened on every single day since you first discovered them. 50% means you listened on half the days. Capped at 100%.
